Jhin

Basic Information

  • Role: Marksman
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Primary Weapon: Whisper (Sniper Rifle)
  • Resource: Mana

Abilities

  • Passive: Whisper - Jhin's hand cannon has four bullets, the fourth one critically strikes and deals bonus damage.
  • Q: Dancing Grenade - Throws a grenade that can bounce to up to four targets, dealing increasing damage with each bounce if it kills.
  • W: Deadly Flourish - Jhin fires a long-range shot that roots the first enemy champion hit if they were recently struck by Jhin or his allies.
  • E: Captive Audience - Places traps that slow enemies and explode after a short delay.
  • R: Curtain Call - Jhin channels to fire up to four super long-range shots, slowing and dealing massive damage on the final shot.

Lore

Jhin, the Virtuoso, is a meticulous criminal psychopath who believes murder is an art.
