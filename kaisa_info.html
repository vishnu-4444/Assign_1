Kai'Sa

Basic Information

  • Role: Marksman
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Primary Weapon: Void Cannon
  • Resource: Mana

Abilities

  • Passive: Second Skin - Kai'Sa's auto attacks apply plasma stacks, which burst for bonus damage after multiple hits.
  • Q: Icathian Rain - Kai'Sa releases a swarm of missiles that evenly strike nearby targets.
  • W: Void Seeker - Fires a long-range beam of Void energy, marking the first enemy hit and revealing them.
  • E: Supercharge - Kai'Sa charges up, gaining movement speed and then attacking rapidly for a short time.
  • R: Killer Instinct - Kai'Sa dashes to a location near a marked enemy champion, shielding herself on arrival.

Lore

Kai'Sa emerged from the void to become a hunter, adapting and surviving against all odds.
