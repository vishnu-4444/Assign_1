Dr. Mundo

Basic Information

  • Role: Fighter
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Primary Weapon: Cleaver
  • Resource: Health (uses health to cast abilities)

Abilities

  • Passive: Goes Where He Pleases - Dr. Mundo regenerates health over time and can resist the first immobilizing effect he is hit by.
  • Q: Infected Bonesaw - Dr. Mundo throws a cleaver, dealing damage based on the target's current health and slowing them.
  • W: Heart Zapper - Dr. Mundo deals damage to nearby enemies and stores a portion of damage taken as gray health, which he can later heal.
  • E: Blunt Force Trauma - Dr. Mundo gains bonus attack damage and deals bonus damage based on missing health with his next basic attack.
  • R: Maximum Dosage - Dr. Mundo restores a large portion of his missing health over time and gains bonus movement speed and attack damage.

Lore

Dr. Mundo, a madman of Zaun, uses his brutal strength and scientific experimentation to spread chaos wherever he goes.
