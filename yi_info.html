Master Yi

Basic Information

  • Role: Assassin
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Primary Weapon: Wuju Blade
  • Resource: Mana

Abilities

  • Passive: Double Strike - Every few basic attacks, Master Yi strikes twice.
  • Q: Alpha Strike - Master Yi dashes to up to four targets, dealing damage and becoming untargetable during the dash.
  • W: Meditate - Master Yi channels and heals himself over time, reducing incoming damage.
  • E: Wuju Style - Temporarily grants bonus true damage to Master Yi's basic attacks.
  • R: Highlander - Increases Master Yi's movement speed and attack speed. Kills and assists extend its duration.

Lore

Master Yi has tempered his body and sharpened his mind in the pursuit of a deeper understanding of the Wuju martial arts.
